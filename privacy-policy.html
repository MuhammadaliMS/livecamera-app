Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2024

Summary: LiveCamera does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how LiveCamera ("we", "our", or "the app"), developed by Zerowing Studios, handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

LiveCamera is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts
  • Does not store any user information on external servers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services

3. Camera and Network Usage

LiveCamera requires access to:

  • Camera: To capture video from your device's camera and stream it to your PC. Video is transmitted directly over your local WiFi network and is never stored or sent to external servers.
  • WiFi/Network: To establish a direct connection between your phone and PC over your local network. No internet connection is required for the core functionality.

4. Data Transmission

All video streaming occurs directly between your phone and your PC over your local WiFi network. The data:

  • Never leaves your local network
  • Is not stored on any servers
  • Is not accessible to Zerowing Studios or any third parties
  • Is transmitted in real-time and not recorded by the app

5. Third-Party Services

LiveCamera does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

LiveCamera does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any users of any age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@zerowing.in
  • GitHub: github.com/MuhammadaliMS/livecamera-app

Your Privacy Matters: LiveCamera is built by a solo developer who believes in respecting user privacy. We will never collect, sell, or share your data because we simply don't have any.
